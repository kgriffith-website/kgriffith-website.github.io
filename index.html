    About Kayla

    Kayla Griffith is a Chicago-area actress. A recent graduate of Northwestern University's Theatre program, she loves acting for both the stage and screen. When not playing make-believe for a living, she can be found in the dance studio, browsing her local farmer's market, or saving memes to her phone. She maintains that even the largest and oldest of dogs can be called a "puppy".
